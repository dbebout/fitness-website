M-W-F Full Body Strength Workout

Train three days per week with this balanced strength routine. Each exercise uses 2 sets, with higher reps for muscle endurance and stability.

Exercise Sets Reps
Dumbbell Press 2 15
Cable Press 2 15
Pull Down 2 15
Tricep Extension - Rope 2 15
Bicep Curls - Dumbbells 2 15
Shoulder Press - Dumbbells 2 15
Squat - Barbell 2 15
Deadlift - Barbell 2 15
Calf Raises 2 20
Plank 2 30 secs
