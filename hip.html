Hip Mobility Routine

Use this hip-focused routine to improve mobility, stability, and strength around the hips. Perform each exercise for 2 sets of 15 reps unless noted otherwise.

Exercise Sets Reps
90/90 Stretch 2 15
Shin Box 90/90 with Dumbbell 2 15
Sissy Squat (use blocks) 2 15
Single Leg RDL 2 15
Lateral Squat 2 15
Bulgarian Split Squat 2 15
