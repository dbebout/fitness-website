About Don Bebout

My name is Don Bebout, and I've spent my life pursuing excellence in business, athletics, education, and personal development.

I grew up in Sweet Home, Oregon, where I graduated from Sweet Home High School in 1977, finishing in the top 10% of my class. Athletics played a major role in my early years, earning First Team All-State honors in football as both a running back and safety, and finishing second in the state wrestling championships during my senior year with a 35-1 record.

My passion for learning continued through college, where I earned a Bachelor of Science degree in Accounting, followed by a Master's Degree in Finance from the University of Southern California in 1991.

In 1992, I co-founded Abracon Corporation, helping build the company into a successful global organization over the next two decades. In 2016, we successfully sold Abracon to a private equity firm, marking the culmination of an incredible entrepreneurial journey.

Outside of business, I've always believed in challenging myself and pursuing new goals. I earned a Black Belt in Tae Kwon Do and later developed a passion for golf, eventually finishing second in the nation in amateur competition.

Of all my accomplishments, the one I value most is my family. I married my wife on August 21, 1982, and together we've built a life centered on commitment, perseverance, and shared experiences.

Today, I remain dedicated to continuous learning, health, investing, golf, personal projects, and helping others achieve their goals. This website serves as a place to share ideas, experiences, and lessons learned throughout a lifetime of business, sports, and personal growth.
